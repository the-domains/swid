Abe’s Market Acquired!

Abe's Market was one of the early market places for natural and organic products. I It was in production at http://www.abesmarket.com and as architect and development lead, I designed an early cloud solution on Amazon Web Services and CentOS for high availability and scalability. I also deployed a uniform development environment, issue management system, SVN, and led Magento development.

I check in on past projects from time to time and have been delighted to witness the success of Abe’s Market. This week their brand was acquired. I recall admiring the founders’ shift and focus on what they believed in after many years at a large fortune 500 healthcare company, that some might have confused as industrial chemical company ^_^
